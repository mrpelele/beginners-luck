LEAGUE OF LEGENDS

Classified as a "Multiplayer Online Battle Arena", League of Legends is a game of strategy, patience and reflexes where you combine different skills, be it knowledge or mechanical prowess, to defeat the enemy team in an objective based match. Choose between over 150 characters, customize their powers and enter the summoner's rift to engage in a 5vs5 combat in a race to see who destroys the enemy's base first

What's this about

League is a very complex game with a lot of information and complicated inner workings. To start, you will experiment with those said mechanics in a casual environment where people will be on your same situation. Don't be afraid to choose whatever looks and sounds best. Slowly, you will begin to understand how everything works

This is the summoner's rift

You will spend most of your time here, be it practicing or playing

Your objective is to destroy the enemy's nexus while protecting your own

For that you will need to go through their defences and stop them from going through yours
