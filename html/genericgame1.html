ROCKET LEAGUE

Vehicular soccer. ¿Sound pretty weird right? Rocket League is a game that combines rocket powered cars with the well known sport to create a never seen before Esport where two teams compete in an all out pure mechanically-skilled face off. Take control of the car you want, practice and master a never ending amount of different skills and get to the field to face other players in a casual or competitive environment, be it alone or with friends

What's this about

With different game modes ranging from 1vs1 duels to 4vs4 chaotic battlefields, casual matches or experimental game modes with special mechanics, rocket league is a flexible game with potential to be a party game or a super competitive environment, aside from your objective, everyone starts at the same point, that means that you first have to understand the basic intricacies of the game
