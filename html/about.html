ABOUT US

I love playing video games, but in these days of competitive multiplayer and player vs player games sometimes it gets harder and harder to get into any game that picks you interest. My objective with this page is to give a super basic instruction manual so anyone can have an easier time when starting any of this games

"¿why should I trust you?"

I've been playing for a very long time, tried every kind of game you could think of. FPS, third person, rts, fighting games, MMOs, RPG, CRPG, 4X, simulators, racing games, mobas, roguelikes, experimental games and a veeeery long etc.

"¿why did you want to do this?"

I like helping people, everytime I saw someone having problems in-game it would come naturally from me to help them in any shape or form I can

"¿what do you get from this?"

absolutely nothing at all, this project is for free
