RAINBOW SIX SIEGE

Take control of an Operator in this tactical first person shooter where you and 4 teammates will fight against another team of 5 in a close-quarter objective based break-in. Attack or defend a defined objective, execute the perfect assault or build the ultimate defence, interact with highly destructive maps utilizing specialized gadgets exclusive to each operator and utilize a big repertoire of weapons and tools that accommodate to your preferred playstyle

What's this about

Rainbow six simulates a training session of different police groups around the world. Each one of this individuals posses specialized skills that made them develop certain techniques and gadgets to perform their job the best they can. Choose one of those characters and learn the basics of the game. With time, you will catch the feel of how weapons and items work, we will still give you a basic crash-course to push you in the right direction




There is no standard map in Rainbow six

You will go through lots of different environments

Your objective is to defuse or protect the bomb

For that you will need to go through their defences or stop them from going through yours
